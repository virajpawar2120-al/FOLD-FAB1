Who We Are

Fold Fab is a specialized brand focused on designing and manufacturing premium customized steel wardrobes for modern Indian homes. Built on the principles of durability, precision, and clean design, we create wardrobe solutions that balance strength with everyday elegance.

With a strong presence across Mumbai, Thane, and surrounding regions, Fold Fab has successfully delivered customized steel wardrobes for a wide range of residential spaces. Each project reflects our commitment to quality craftsmanship, thoughtful space planning, and long-term reliability.

We work exclusively with mild steel, offering a superior alternative to conventional wooden wardrobes. Our wardrobes are designed to withstand time, usage, and changing lifestyles — while maintaining a refined, contemporary finish.

At Fold Fab, we focus on understanding client requirements in detail and executing every project with accuracy and care. From design to installation, our goal is simple: deliver wardrobes that meet the highest standards and exceed expectations.
